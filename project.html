SkySense Family: A Series Works of Remote Sensing Foundation Model for Earth Observation

Overview

The SkySense family represents a comprehensive suite of remote sensing foundation models designed to address various challenges in earth observation. These models leverage advanced multi-modal learning techniques to interpret complex geospatial data with unprecedented accuracy.

From universal interpretation to open-world understanding, and from multi-modal integration to fine-grained instruction tuning, the SkySense series continues to push the boundaries of what's possible in remote sensing AI research and applications.
