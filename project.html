SkySense Family: A Series Works of Remote Sensing Foundation Model for Earth Observation

Overview

Welcome to SkySense family project, a series of remote sensing (RS) foundation models for earth observation. We start exploring RS foundation models from Prompt Encoder (SkySense++ and SkySense-O), RS Encoder (SkySense and SkySense-V2), and RS Decoder (SkySenseGPT), turning satellite data into actionable insights for disaster management, environmental monitoring, and more.
